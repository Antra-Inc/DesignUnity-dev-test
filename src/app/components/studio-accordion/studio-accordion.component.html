Hyderabad Experience Center

Design Unity’s Experience Center is a destination of choice for those who appreciate the timeless beauty of Hyderabad. Covering 15000 square feet, it’s a comprehensive showcase of a wide variety of stunning surface finishes that bring the mesmerizing vibe of this South Indian city to your home or office. The center is designed to give you a tangible sense of the city of pearl with its colors and textures that bring Hyderabad to life anywhere in the world. It’s a place that brings the ancient, royal culture of the city to your walls, ceilings, and floors. Step into the ravishing Mughal culture impeccably depicted in our products, by pushing the boundaries of design.

Visit us on the 10th floor, Techno 1, Opposite KIMS Hospital, Raidurgam, Hyderabad, and start your journey towards creating beautiful spaces.

What you’ll See

Our Experience Center is home to 32 unique finishes representing Hyderabad and the largest collection of Venetian Plasters in India. Explore the beauty of exquisite finishes, each taking you into a different world of Hyderabadi opulence.
